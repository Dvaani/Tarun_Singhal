About

Thirty years of building what lasts

I'm a high-performance operator with an entrepreneurially-minded approach to growth — the kind of leader who sees an untapped market before it becomes obvious, then builds the engine to capture it.

Across Global Capability Centers, enterprise sales, and digital transformation, I've taken organisations from zero to multi-million-dollar market share — including growing GCC operations in India for UK, European, and US businesses at Airbus and Société Générale, representing tens of thousands of employees.

My edge is trust. CXOs and senior influencers across telecom, government, energy, banking, and professional services know that when I enter a room, the conversation shifts from vendor to partner. That's not a claim — it's a career.

Today, I operate at the frontier where GenAI meets human judgment — designing operating models that make geography irrelevant and talent universally accessible.
